Vortex mathematics

This is a simple Dart app created to explore some of the topics discussed in the Mathologer YouTube video, Tesla's 3-6-9 and Vortex Math.

Path cycles are identified by repeatedly multiplying node indices by the specified multiplier and then connecting them to the result's remainder when divided by modulus.

You can play with the values of these two parameters below.
